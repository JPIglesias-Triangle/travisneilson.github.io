This child here
he has so much potential
hidden under need
Pick me up
change my pants, feed me
this is life.
you can walk
but you can never leave
I’m not looking
Goodbye house
i am going away now
goodbye dad
Dancing in the spring
lost in the tall grass
those silly days
these are mine
you can not have them
get your own
I was sick
had to do extra homework
yes, I cried
look at you
blonde hair in your eyes
you see me?
we are funny
we always like to laugh
tell me jokes
i think about
all the things that we
could yet become
tell me again
tell me that i’m not
what you want
I’m bigger now
I am cool like you
I’ll follow you
we are brothers
we will always be friends
more than that
what the hell?
no one told me about
the strange things
this is trouble
this will stay with me
it is unavoidable
close my eyes
the world fades to black
I’m asleep now
Let me see
in to those dark places
we are blind
This is rage
take what mess we have
make it beautiful
a new secret
please tell me more stories
we are free
cleaned up now
we all wear suits and
make good choices
I can help
I can do good and
make a difference
I’m back again
I don’t fit in anywhere
I’ll run away
young and silly
think you know things but
you still don’t
I love you
I know you love me
all I need
this is hard
I thought I understood you
I’m not happy
I’m not afraid
but I don’t know what
I am anymore
get a plan
find a way to be
be the best
Everything always changes
nothing ever makes sense
hang in there
my little girl
you’re a precious little thing
in my hands
you have it
now is your time, finally
are you there?
My son Eames,
are you ready to live?
I’ll be here
I can help
again, I found a way
to help people
I, us, we —
We have found a place
to be happy
