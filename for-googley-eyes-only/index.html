I'm just trying to
make something great
before I die.

Travis Neilson

Hi Google. Let me tell you a story.

Two days after we buried my Grandfather, my little girl came into the world. That was 5 years ago. I remember it clearly because the juxtaposition of those two events seemed so poignant to me.

Shortly thereafter I wrote an article on my website titled “Spheres of Influence”, in which I concluded that “to truly live an extraordinary life, you must strive to reach and influence as many people as deeply and as profoundly as you possibly can.”

Since then I have done my best to do exactly that. I have sought to mentor and lead where appropriate, I have tried make sure the work that I created was always the kind that I was proud of, that helped others pursue their own goals. Recently I’ve taken on a more public persona; teaching and motivating the next generation of creators through producing videos, podcasts, and articles that are intended to teach professionalism and promote creative health.

I see this opportunity to discuss a future with Google as an extension of this guiding principal. Google is a company famous for many things, and among them creating tools that uplift and push the human experience further. Googles reach is unparalleled in executing these projects. This aligns perfectly with my values.

Finally, like many Googlers whom I have had the pleasure of knowing: I’m just trying to make something great before I die.

Thank you for taking the time to have these discussions with me.
